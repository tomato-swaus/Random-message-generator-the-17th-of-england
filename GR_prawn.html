Recipe 1: Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Feta

Here's the recipe:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Whisk together eggs and a handful of spinach.
  3. Pour the mixture into the skillet and cook until the eggs are set.
  4. Sprinkle feta cheese on top and serve hot.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. If desired, add chopped tomatoes or bell peppers for extra flavor.
  7. For a creamier texture, add a splash of milk to the egg mixture.
  8. Use fresh spinach for best results, but frozen spinach works too.
  9. Experiment with different types of cheese like goat cheese or cheddar.
  10. Top with fresh herbs like parsley or chives for added freshness.
  11. Serve with toast or crusty bread for a complete breakfast.
  12. For a low-carb option, serve with sliced avocado or a side salad.
  13. Make sure not to overcook the eggs; they should be soft and slightly creamy.
  14. Feel free to add cooked bacon or sausage for a heartier meal.
  15. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious scrambled eggs with spinach and feta!

15-Minute Breakfast Burrito Recipe

Recipe:

Here's how to make a delicious breakfast burrito in just 15 minutes:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of oil.
  2. Crack 2 eggs into the skillet and scramble them until cooked through.
  3. Add diced bell peppers, onions, and any other desired veggies to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. Warm a large tortilla in the microwave for 10-15 seconds or until soft and pliable.
  5. Place the warmed tortilla on a plate and spoon the scrambled eggs and veggie mixture onto the center of the tortilla.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the eggs and veggies.
  7. Drizzle salsa or hot sauce over the cheese, if desired.
  8. Optional: Add cooked bacon or sausage for extra flavor.
  9. Roll the tortilla tightly around the filling, tucking in the sides as you go.
  10. Place the burrito seam-side down on the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side, or until lightly browned and crispy.
  11. Remove the burrito from the skillet and let it cool slightly before serving.
  12. Serve the breakfast burrito with additional salsa, sour cream, or avocado on the side, if desired.
  13. Enjoy your delicious and satisfying breakfast burrito!

15-Minute Blueberry Pancakes

Recipe:

Here's the recipe:

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (don't overmix).
  4. Gently fold in fresh blueberries.
  5. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  6. Scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form and edges are firm, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  8. Repeat with remaining batter.
  9. Serve hot with maple syrup and extra blueberries, if desired.
